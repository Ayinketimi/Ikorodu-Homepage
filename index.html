Visit Ikorodu

Enjoy the unique blend of urban living and traditional culture in a vibrant city located in Lagos state of Nigeria.

Top three activities to do at Ikorodu

Experience and purchase works of art with cultural heritage

The Ojuloge Arts and Cultural Center has a gallery where you can view and purchase works of art by local artists, including paintings, sculptures, and textiles. It also has a theater where you can watch performances, including plays, dance performances, and musical concerts.

Go swim, sunbathe and relax

Ikorodu beach is a great place to swim, sunbathe, and relax. It is also a popular spot for fishing and water sports. The beach is known for its tranquil atmosphere and beautiful scenery. It is surrounded by lush green vegetation and has clear blue water, making it an ideal spot for swimming and sunbathing. Visitors can also rent boats and kayaks to explore the lagoon or go fishing.

Learn about the history of Ikorodu and the yoruba people

Ikorodu heritage museum has a collection of artifacts, including traditional clothing, musical instruments, and household items. The museum was established in 2009 with the aim of preserving and promoting the history and cultural heritage of Ikorodu and the Yoruba people.
